About the Project

Declining green cover is increasing pollution levels in urban areas. Our project focuses on tree plantation, public awareness, and sustainable living practices.

  • Organizing tree plantation drives
  • Eco-awareness campaigns in schools and communities
  • Promoting waste segregation and sustainability
  • Volunteer engagement for clean-up efforts
